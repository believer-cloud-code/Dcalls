📱 Getting Started

Dcalls is a next-generation communication platform that enables crystal-clear voice calls, stunning 4K video calls, and instant messaging over the internet. It's powered by Damai, an intelligent AI assistant that handles translations, scheduling, smart replies, voice cloning, and more.
Download the Dcalls app from the App Store or Google Play, or visit our web app. Sign up using your email address or phone number. You'll receive a verification code — enter it, set your display name, and you're ready to start calling within 60 seconds.
Dcalls is available on iOS, Android, Windows, macOS, and Web. Your conversations sync seamlessly across all devices. The web version requires no download — just log in at the Dcalls web app and start calling.

🤖 Damai AI Help

Damai uses advanced neural machine translation models that process speech in real-time during calls. As someone speaks, Damai translates and delivers the message in your preferred language with near-zero latency. It currently supports 50+ languages including Spanish, Mandarin, Arabic, French, Hindi, and more. You can enable it during any call by tapping the Damai icon and selecting your target language.
Absolutely. Voice cloning requires explicit user consent and a secure voice sample enrollment process (you read a short passage aloud). Your voice model is encrypted end-to-end with AES-256 and never shared with third parties. It's stored exclusively on your device by default, with optional encrypted cloud backup. You can delete your voice model permanently at any time from Settings > Damai > Voice Model > Delete.
Simply tell Damai "Schedule a call with [contact name] for [time/day]" or type it in chat. Damai checks both calendars, confirms the slot, and sends invitations automatically. You'll get a push notification 10 minutes before the call. You can also say "Damai, what's on my schedule today?" for a quick overview.
Yes! After every call where Damai is active, you'll receive an AI-generated summary including key decisions, action items with assigned owners, important timestamps, and follow-up suggestions. Summaries are saved in your chat history and can be exported as PDF or shared with participants. Enable this in Settings > Damai > Meeting Summaries.
Voice commands let you control Dcalls hands-free. Say "Hey Damai" followed by your command — for example: "Hey Damai, call Mom," "Hey Damai, send a message to the team group," or "Hey Damai, mute my mic." Voice commands work even when Dcalls is in the background (with permission). You can customize the wake word in Settings > Damai > Voice Commands.
Voice cloning creates a personalized AI voice model that sounds just like you. To set it up: Go to Settings > Damai > Voice Cloning > Enroll. You'll be asked to read 10-15 sentences aloud in a quiet environment. Damai processes this sample (takes about 2 minutes) and creates your voice model. Once ready, your smart replies and voice command responses will use your cloned voice. Premium feature.

🔐 Account & Billing

Go to Settings > Account > Change Password, or click "Forgot Password" on the login screen. You'll receive a reset link via email. For security, the link expires after 30 minutes. If you don't see the email, check your spam folder or contact us at dcallsapp@gmail.com.
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. For annual business plans, we also support invoicing and wire transfers. All payments are processed securely through Stripe.
You can cancel anytime from Settings > Billing > Manage Subscription > Cancel. Your premium features remain active until the end of your current billing period. We don't offer partial refunds, but you won't be charged again after cancellation. Your data is retained for 90 days in case you want to reactivate.
Yes. All Dcalls communications — voice, video, and messages — are protected with end-to-end encryption (E2EE) by default. This means only you and the intended recipients can read or hear your conversations. Not even Dcalls can access your call content. We use the Signal Protocol, the gold standard for messaging encryption.

🔧 Technical Troubleshooting

Try these steps: (1) Switch to a stronger Wi-Fi or cellular connection, (2) Close background apps that may be using bandwidth, (3) In Settings > Call, toggle "Adaptive Quality" on — this automatically adjusts video resolution based on your connection, (4) If on mobile, try moving to a location with better signal. Dcalls works with as little as 256kbps for voice calls.
