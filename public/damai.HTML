Six powerful capabilities that transform how you communicate.

Voice Commands

Control Dcalls completely hands-free. Start calls, send messages, and manage your conversations using just your voice. Perfect for when you're driving or multitasking.

Break every language barrier. Damai translates conversations in real-time across 50+ languages, making global communication effortless and natural.
